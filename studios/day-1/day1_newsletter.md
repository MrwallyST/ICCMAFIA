### 4. The Three Market States
The market only knows how to do three things. Before you worry about "crossovers," "hizzys," or "shazams," you must learn to "dribble" by identifying these states.

#### Uptrend
The market is a "staircase going up." It moves with a clear, upward purpose, respecting its own structural integrity as it climbs.

#### Downtrend
The market is a "staircase going down." It moves with a clear, downward purpose, steadily losing value while following a predictable path.

#### Consolidation
This is the "rule breaker" or the "shady friend." In this state, the market refuses to choose a direction and lacks any predictable pattern.

### 5. Trend Identification Mechanics (The HH/HL/LH/LL System)
To confirm a trend, you must identify **Pivots**. A pivot is the highest or lowest point price reaches before it shifts direction. Use these signatures to verify the health of the market:

*   **The Uptrend Signature:**
    *   **Higher High (HH):** Price pushes past the old peak, reaching a new highest point.
    *   **Higher Low (HL):** A pullback occurs, but it stays strictly above the previous low point.
*   **The Downtrend Signature:**
    *   **Lower High (LH):** A temporary price increase that fails to reach the previous peak.
    *   **Lower Low (LL):** Price falls past the previous low, reaching a new lowest point.

### 6. Consolidation: The "No-Trade" Zone
Consolidation is "Public Enemy Number One." In this state, price action is a choppy, sideways mess that breaks the rules of structure.

> **Warning**
> You must pick your markets like you pick your friends. You wouldn't hang out with criminals who break the law; do not trade in a market that breaks its own rules. Identifying marks include:
> *   Sideways, messy movement with no clear direction.
> *   Price poking up to a new high, then immediately crashing to break the previous low.
> *   A total lack of predictable HH/HL or LH/LL patterns.
>
> **Directive: When rules are broken, walk away. Protecting your capital and your sanity is the priority.**
