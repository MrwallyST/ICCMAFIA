Uptrend,A market condition where price continues to reach higher points and establishes a pattern of growth.,Consists of higher highs (price reaches a point higher than previous) and higher lows (pullbacks that stay higher than previous lows).,Trade with the trend; identify higher highs and higher lows to confirm the structure follows the rules.,"Like life getting higher and higher; price makes a high, then a higher high, and a higher low that is above the previous low.",[1]
Downtrend,"A market condition where price is in a state of decline, moving lower over time.",Consists of lower lows and lower highs; price breaks previous lows to create new lower points.,Trade when price is following the rules of lower highs and lower lows; stay on higher time frames for stronger results.,"Price reaches a certain decline and pivots; if it breaks the previous low, it becomes a lower low.",[1]
Consolidation,"A market state where price moves sideways without a clear direction, breaking previous highs and lows inconsistently.",Highs and lows are broken back and forth; price has no rules and moves sideways rather than in a trend.,Do not trade; avoid markets that are breaking rules and lack direction.,Like being around criminals who break rules; stay away from the market when it breaks structure and shows no clear trend.,[1]
